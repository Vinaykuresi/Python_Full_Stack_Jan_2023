            a) >

    3) Logical Operator : 
            a) AND

        AND : 

            A   B   A and B
            T   T   T
            T   F   F
            F   T   F
            F   F   F

        OR : 

            A   B   A or B
            T   T   T
            T   F   T
            F   T   T
            F   F   F

        Not : 

            A       Not A
            T       F
            F       T

        Not(Not(F)) -> F
